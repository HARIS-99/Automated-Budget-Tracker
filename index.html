Welcome to My Rust CLI Tool!

A powerful and simple command-line tool to manage your budget efficiently.

Features:

Add Income & Expenses

Easily add income and expenses and track your budget in real time.

Track Budget Overrun

Get alerted if your expenses exceed your budget so you can stay on track.

Simple and Fast

Our tool is lightweight and runs seamlessly on your command line.

How It Works:

This tool allows you to manage your income, expenses, and track your budget directly from the terminal. It's simple to use and helps you stay on top of your finances.
